Water Roller / Filter Barrel

Video Demonstrating Fluid Flow

 In my university design project, I worked with a team to create a water rolling barrel that helps provide clean water to communities in Africa. Our goal was to assist with one of the United Nations' global goals, and we felt that this innovative solution could make a real difference in communities that rely on traditional methods of obtaining water.
 The water rolling barrel is a simple yet effective device that is designed to be rolled along the ground, much like a traditional barrel. As it rolls, the water inside is filtered, ensuring that it is clean and safe to drink. We focused on designing the barrel to be as efficient as possible, using advanced fluid simulations to optimize the flow of water through the filtering system.
 I was responsible for leading the design efforts on the project and working on the fluid simulations, while my team focused on the technical aspects and conducting research. We were able to complete the project within the allotted time frame. Overall, it was a rewarding experience that allowed me to apply my skills and knowledge to make a positive impact on the world.
